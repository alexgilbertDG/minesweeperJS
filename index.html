Watch out for the mine !

In this game you need to shovel through the ground to find gold, but be careful, there is mine in the field.

To collect gold you need to click on it and hold the click for 2 seconds. Each piece of gold collected equal more points.

To win you need to put a flag on each possible mine.

The number in the square represent the number of mine surronding this square. The difficulty will change the board size, the number of mines, how many space you can see and the number of gold.

Which difficulty do you want to play:

Easy - Intermediaire - Hard - Expert
